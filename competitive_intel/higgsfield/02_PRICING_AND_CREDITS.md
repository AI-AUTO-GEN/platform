## Credit Consumption System
- Credits are deducted **dynamically** based on:
  - Model selected (e.g., Kling 3.0 costs more than Nano Banana)
  - Video duration
  - Resolution (720p, 1080p, 4K)
  - Features used (camera movements, upscaling)
- **No credit rollover** between billing periods
- **Unlimited models:** Higher tiers get "unlimited" access to mid-tier models (no credit cost)

## Pro Workflow Tip
Experienced users draft on **unlimited/cheap models** first, then commit credits to final renders on premium models. This is the optimal cost management strategy.

## Annual vs Monthly
Significant discounts for annual billing. The prices above are annual. Monthly pricing is typically 30-50% higher.
