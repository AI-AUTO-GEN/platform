## Product Lines

### 1. Collab (Main Generation Workspace)
The primary workspace for text-to-image and text-to-video generation, accessible at `/generate`.

### 2. Cinema Studio (v3.5)
Professional-grade cinematic video production environment:
- **Camera Control:** Focal length, lens selection, aperture, sensor settings
- **Movement Presets:** Dolly in/out, crane, orbit, crash zoom, FPV sweeps, bullet time
- **Keyframing:** Multiple keyframe points for camera path, speed, timing
- **Character Consistency:** Soul ID for cross-shot identity lock
- **Mr. Higgs:** AI co-director that breaks down scenes into shots
- **Multi-Shot Sequencing:** Design full scenes, not just isolated clips
- **Reusable Elements:** Characters, locations, props tagged for cross-shot reference

### 3. Marketing Studio (NEW)
Automated ad generation from product/app URLs:
- **URL-to-Ad Workflow:** Paste URL → auto-extract brand assets → generate video ads
- **Templates:** Hyper Motion, Unboxing, UGC, Virtual Try-On, TV Spot, Tutorials
- **AI Avatars:** 40+ ready-to-use, plus custom avatar generation
- **Engines:** Seedance 2.0 (native lip-sync, physics-aware), Soul 2.0 (avatars)
- **Formats:** 9:16, 16:9, 1:1 up to 1080p

### 4. Originals
AI-generated series/content showcasing platform capabilities (e.g., "Mork" series).

### 5. Apps Library (One-Click Tools)
Massive library of specialized micro-apps:

**Professional:**
- Similarity Score, Expand Image, Angles 2.0 Pro, Shots, Transitions

**Enhance & Style:**

**Face & Identity:**

**Games & Characters:**

### 6. Assist (AI Chat)

### 7. Community
